BIO

After graduating with a degree in Biological Science in 2013, I began my career with BP, where I discovered a passion for working with data and technology. Although I had initially aimed to work for an international trading company, I found my time at BP to be incredibly fulfilling. Working with various data and documents, I honed my technical skills by experimenting with different software and tools.

Driven by my passion for learning and developing new skillsets, I enrolled in the "Cognitive AI bot development program," where I gained hands-on experience with SQL, Java, Hadoop, AWS, and more. Through the program, I developed a mobile app, website, and chatbot using Raspberry Pi.

Upon completing the course, I returned to work and expanded my scope of responsibilities by utilizing all the software and tools at my disposal. My efforts resulted in significant positive changes at work, and I was soon offered a job at another company, where I continued to learn and develop my technical skills.

Throughout my career, I have gained valuable experience working in diverse and multicultural environments. I have honed my project management skills and leadership abilities, allowing me to effectively deliver high-quality projects on time. My proven track record of delivering results has enabled me to navigate complex challenges and provide effective solutions.

While working, I also co-authored a book about big data and data science, which has been published in its 3rd edition, which is a part of expanding my self-development.
I am passionate about studying and keeping busy to develop myself more all the time. Driven by a desire to continually improve and expand my skill set, I am currently pursuing an MSc in Data Science at the University of Manchester. Although I have not yet completed many projects, I am confident in my potential for growth and development and am eager to continue learning and expanding my skills.

As a dedicated and lifelong learner, I am passionate about using my skills and experience to drive success in future projects. I am excited to see where my career will take me and believe that this portfolio website serves as a testament to my dedication and commitment to professional growth.
